Start sharing location


What about privacy?

Yes, your location is sent to the internet (via https) but your location is never stored long term on the server. Currently it's completely discarded 4 hours after the last location update was received from your device. (Automated clean-up needs to be reimplemented)

Will there be an App?

Maybe, ... the API already exists.

Notes for location access by websites on Android (Chrome, Firefox) and maybe also iOS

  • Your screen must be active
  • The browser tab needs to be in the foreground
